How to use this playbook

A self-serve technical companion for MBB case and fit preparation — and a structured drilling tool for your coaching sessions. Work it in this order:

#StepWhy
1Start with fit and motivationYour "why consulting / why this firm" answer anchors every interview. Get it credible first.
2Build a consulting story bank5–7 PEI stories cover leadership, impact, conflict, failure, ambiguity. Reuse and re-angle them.
3Learn core frameworks — then customizeKnow them cold so you can build a tailored structure, not recite a template.
4Practice market sizing and math dailySpeed and clean narration are trained, not innate. Short daily reps beat cramming.
5Complete case prompts by categoryWork one archetype at a time until the structure is automatic.
6Practice out loud with the voice toolInterviews are spoken. Record, listen back, cut filler.
7Generate an AI coaching prompt after each repPaste your answer into the generator, run it in ChatGPT/Claude, get targeted feedback.
8Track readiness by competencyLog feedback scores in the dashboard to see which dimensions still lag.
9Repeat weak categoriesLet the dashboard, not your comfort zone, choose what you drill next.
10Prepare partner-level answersFinal rounds reward judgment and maturity. Rehearse the harder, more ambiguous version.

Final rounds

Partner round preparation

Final rounds differ from first rounds: prompts are more ambiguous, the case flows more conversationally, and partners weight judgment, maturity, and fit far more heavily. Expect pushback — and be ready to defend a recommendation without becoming defensive. Communicate like a consultant advising a client, not a student answering a quiz.

Sample partner-round questions
  1. Why consulting now?
  2. Why our firm specifically?
  3. Tell me about a time you changed someone's mind.
  4. Tell me about a time you failed.
  5. What would your teammates say is hard about working with you?
  6. What business problem are you most interested in solving?
  7. If you were advising the CEO, what would you do first?
  8. What risks would you worry about?
  9. How would you align stakeholders?
  10. What would you do if the client disagreed?

MBB Case Prompt Library

A complete consulting case-practice library for McKinsey, Bain, BCG and other top firms. Each prompt is built to drill problem structuring, clarifying questions, hypothesis development, market sizing, profitability analysis, exhibit interpretation, consulting math, synthesis, and final recommendations.

Module 03

Consulting Case Frameworks

The structured problem-solving approaches every consulting candidate should know cold — for case interviews, market sizing, profitability, growth, pricing, M&A, operations, behavioral questions, and final recommendations. Frameworks are raw material for a custom structure, never a script to recite.

Module 04

Consulting Math & Analytics Drills

The math, data interpretation, and business judgment required for elite consulting interviews. The goal is to perform these clearly and quickly aloud, narrating your logic. Each drill gives the formula, a consulting-style example, a worked calculation, how to verbalize it, and the common mistakes.

Module 05

Consulting Fit, Behavioral & PEI Preparation

MBB interviews evaluate more than case performance. Candidates must show leadership, drive, resilience, personal impact, teamwork, maturity, executive presence, and genuine motivation for consulting. Each prompt gives a strong answer, a story structure, what the interviewer is testing, red flags, and how to elevate it for MBB.

Module 06

MBB Interview Battlecard

Your quick-reference cheat sheet. Review before every consulting coffee chat, recruiter call, case screen, first-round, final-round, and partner interview.

Module 07

Consulting Interview Red Flags

The specific answers and behaviors that weaken candidates — memorized frameworks, weak structuring, unclear math, poor synthesis, shallow business judgment, vague fit stories, and failure to connect analysis to client impact. Know them, then design your reps to avoid them.

Module 08

Your readiness is tracked from your actual practice — not self-scored. After each rep, generate the AI coaching prompt, run it in ChatGPT or Claude, then paste the reply back in the practice window.
